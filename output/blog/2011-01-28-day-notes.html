Day notes - Fri 28th Jan 2011

Tom and I paired on fixing our CI machine. The way in which it was re-provisioned meant that certain application dependencies were missing.

I spent some time this afternoon working on a little project that'll allow me to upload my bank transactions to a Google Spreadsheet to track my spending. The code's in the ofx-to-csv repository and currently comprises a script to convert OFX to CSV and a Google Apps Script to remove duplicate transactions from the Google Spreadsheet.
