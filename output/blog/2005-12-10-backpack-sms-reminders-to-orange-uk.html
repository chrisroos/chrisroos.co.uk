Backpack sms reminders to orange uk

I’ve been using the reminders feature of backpack to text me when I need to be doing something time specific. As per this thread in the forum, although I get the text messages, there is no subject; meaning I have to remember what it was that I wanted to be reminded about. Hmmm.

Following the advice from that thread, I just set-up a forwarding rule on my email providers server to forward reminder alerts from backpack to my orange account. This is in place of getting backpack to send directly to my orange email. I now get the subjects in my text messages as expected. Yay.
