Day notes - Wed 27th Oct 2010

Paired with Kalv to investigate some problems with #blue where certain bits of the site didn’t work without Javascript, even though we thought we had tests for exactly the case where javascript was disabled. It turns out that Capybara does some evilness in simulating javascript behaviour when it detects special data-* attributes that Rails emits. Kalv’s written more in Stop being clever Capybara.
