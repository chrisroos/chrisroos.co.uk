dabble db initial thoughts

I’ve been following the blog for dabbledb from smallthought. I was initially excited about the promise of this software having read about it in a post by Chad Fowler. It intends to replace those custom Excel/Access ‘applications’ that are so pervasive in organisations of all sizes.

Today I saw a post on the dabbledb blog containing a link to a movie of the software being used. Completely initial impression with no real knowledge behind it is that it looks far too complicated to take the market for which it is intended.

Having said that, I’ll wait to see more movies and maybe be lucky enough to get to try it in beta before commenting further.
