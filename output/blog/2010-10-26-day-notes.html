Day notes - Tue 26th Oct 2010

Paired with Jase on multiple minor problems we found in hashblue. We’ve decided to fix bugs as we come across them rather than logging them in Pivotal Tracker, and we found a few recently while working on another story.

We discovered that assert_template is slightly more screwed than we’d realised. It turns out that even if checking for a partial with a layout did work, it still wouldn’t do what you’d expect: it’d only check for the layout. We amended our assert_template patch to emit a warning if you do try to use assert_template with the :partial and :layout options.

I started reading the Hardcore Zen book.
