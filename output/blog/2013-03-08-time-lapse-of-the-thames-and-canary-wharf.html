Time Lapse Of The Thames And Canary Wharf

A time-lapse composed of 595 images (1 image every 30 seconds) from 13:40 to 18:43 on Sunday 3rd March 2013.

I took the pictures using LapseIt Pro on my Samsung Galaxy S2 at a resolution of 1920x1080. I turned the images into a movie using iMovie and by following this youtube instructional video.
