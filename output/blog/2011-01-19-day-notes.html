Day notes - Wed 19th Jan 2011

I paired with James M a little on some work he’d done to replace delayed_job with resque-scheduler.

I spent some time investigating shared address book type functionality in Google Apps for your domains. It would appear that there are some paid-for apps in the Apps Marketplace and a Shared Contacts API available to Business and Education GAFYD accounts. I did a little spiking with the API and, although you can create and list ‘shared contacts’ they don’t actually appear in the GMail client or Contact Editor.
