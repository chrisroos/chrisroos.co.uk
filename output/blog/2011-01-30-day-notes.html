Day notes - Sun 30th Jan 2011

I finished listening to An Evening with Ranulph Fiennes. It's a seriously good listen and I'd recommend it to anyone. It's a recording of a talk he gave in front of an audience and it sounds as though there were probably slides to go along with it: I don't think it's made any worse by the lack of slides but I suspect they might've made some of the funnier points even funnier.

I finished reading Hardcore Zen that Jase lent me. I enjoyed it and think I might even understand some of it. It'll definitely require a re-read though.
